Frequently Asked Questions

Are QR codes free to generate?

Yes! Our QR code generator is completely free. No sign-up required, no hidden fees. Generate as many QR codes as you need.

Do QR codes expire?

No, QR codes generated here are permanent. They will work forever as long as the content (URL, WiFi, text) remains valid.

Can I customize QR codes with my logo?

Yes! You can upload your logo or brand image to embed in the center of the QR code. Make sure your logo is square for best results.

What file formats can I download?

You can download your QR code as PNG (raster image) or SVG (vector graphics). SVG is best for printing and scaling.

How do I scan a QR code?

Most smartphones can scan QR codes directly using the camera app. iPhone users can use the Camera app, and Android users can use Google Lens.

Can I embed this QR code on my website?

Yes! Download the PNG or SVG and embed it anywhere on your website. For Google Sites, use the Embed option and paste the image URL.
